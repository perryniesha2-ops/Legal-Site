About Scouta

Scouta helps you quickly find and act on the opportunities that matter—without getting buried in noise. We combine streamlined workflows, clean design, and just-enough automation so you can focus on decisions, not dashboards.

What we do

  • Discovery: pull the signals you care about into one place.
  • Prioritization: lightweight scoring and sorting to surface what’s worth doing now.
  • Action: simple hand-offs and notifications so nothing falls through the cracks.

How it works

Connect the sources you already use, set a few preferences, and let Scouta do the sifting. Your workspace stays fast, private, and easy to share with teammates when you’re ready.

Privacy & security
